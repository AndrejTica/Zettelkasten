- A scrum team consists of a scrum master, product owner and developers.
- They posses all skills together to accomplish a task (interdisciplinary).
- The team organizes itself. Who will do what and when?
- Typically team size is max 10 people. Small enough for good communication and
  big enough to accomplish meaningful tasks. 
- If too big, should be split into multiple scrum teams but they share the PO
- The scrum team is responsible for all activities relating to the product.
- Each spring should have a sustainable speed of work. 
- The whole scrum team is responsible to deliver a working increment each week.

## Developers
Can have many skills but generally should be able to:
- Create a sprint backlog. 
- Keeping quality by fulfilling the definition of done.  

## Product owners
- Developing the product and explicit communication. 
- Create the product backlog
- Define priorities and make the product backlog transparent and understood.

The decision of the PO has to be respected, if there are differing opinions then
changing the backlog will need convincing work.

## Scrum master
Is responsible for keeping up with scrum values and rules as defined. This is
done by education the team of the scrum values. 
Is responsible for the effectiveness of the scrum team. 
- Teaches self organisation and collaboration. 
- Helps keeping the focus on important increments after the definition of done.
- Helps solve impediments. 
- Makes sure all scrum events are being kept and within the timebox. 
- Helps the PO with managing the backlog. 
- Helps defining clear and concise backlog entries. 
- Helps with empiric product planning. 
- Facilitating the collaboration with stakeholders. 
- Teaching about the values of scrum
